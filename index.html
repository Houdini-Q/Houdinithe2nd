Facts About Little Ol' Me

  1. Greetings! Well, for starters my name is Quintez Martin. If one word alone could describe me then that word would most likely be, passionate. I have so much passion and creativity when it comes to crafting works of art. Whether that work of art is making graphical and visual arts to designing and developing webpages, I will always put one hundred percent of my heart, mind and soul into it. So it's safe to say that I am overflowing with good work ethic.

    For nearly half a decade I've been dabbing a little bit in the world of pixel art for 2-D video games on byond.com. One reason is because I aspire to add being a game developer to my mantle of many attributes. I have a confident, cool, and funny yet laid back personality which makes it easy for me to relate to generally any person and form smooth relationships. And above all I love God and because of that I have set principles and moral values that help me govern my self managed lifestyle.
